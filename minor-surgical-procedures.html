Outpatient Oral Surgery

Minor oral surgical procedures are performed in our clinic under completely sterile conditions and local anesthesia. These include standard or surgical extractions of impacted wisdom teeth, minor cyst removals, and cosmetic gum recontouring to correct a 'gummy' smile.

Wisdom teeth often become impacted (trapped under the bone or gums) due to lack of space in the jaw. This can lead to severe pain, swelling, infection, and damage to adjacent molars. Our surgical specialists remove impacted teeth safely with minimal tissue trauma, ensuring a smooth and rapid recovery.

Sterile Operatory Theatre

Patient safety is our highest priority. All surgical procedures are carried out in a dedicated clinical operatory equipped with advanced surgical tools and autoclaved instrumentation conforming to strict international hygiene guidelines.

  • Impacted wisdom tooth surgical extractions
  • Sterile clinical environment for surgeries
  • Minimal recovery downtime with advanced tools
  • Cosmetic gum recontouring and minor cyst removals

Frequently Asked Questions

The procedure is performed under local anesthesia, so you will feel no pain, only pressure. Any mild post-procedure discomfort can be easily managed with prescribed pain relievers and ice packs.

Most patients recover within 3 to 5 days. We provide detailed post-operative instructions (avoiding hot foods, using a straw, etc.) to ensure a quick and complication-free healing process.

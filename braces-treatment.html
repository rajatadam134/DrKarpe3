Orthodontic Realignment for All Ages

Crowded, crooked, or misaligned teeth can affect your self-esteem and make oral hygiene difficult, leading to decay and gum problems. Orthodontic treatment realigns teeth and jaws to improve your bite, facial symmetry, and long-term oral health.

We offer a comprehensive range of orthodontic options tailored to your lifestyle. These include traditional metal brackets, aesthetic tooth-colored ceramic braces, and modern clear invisible aligners that can be removed for eating and brushing.

Advanced Habit Breaking Systems

For children, habits like thumb sucking or mouth breathing can negatively affect jaw development and cause teeth malpositioning. We provide specialized habit-breaking and functional appliances to guide jaw growth in early years.

  • Metal, ceramic, and self-ligating braces
  • Invisible, removable clear aligners (Invisalign)
  • Habit-breaking appliances for pediatric care
  • Improves bite, speech, and jaw muscle alignment

Discreet & Comfortable Teeth Alignment

Clear aligners are custom-made, medical-grade thermoplastic trays designed to straighten misaligned teeth as a modern alternative to traditional braces. They fit snugly over your teeth and apply targeted forces to correct mild-to-moderate crowding, spacing, and minor bite issues.

Why Choose Clear Aligners?

Clear aligners allow you to maintain a professional, natural appearance while undergoing orthodontic care. Because they contain no metal brackets or wires, they are exceptionally comfortable, easy to clean, and do not restrict what you can eat.

  • Clear, transparent design that is virtually invisible to others
  • Smooth, custom-molded plastic prevents cheek and tongue sores
  • Removable for optimal oral hygiene and easy eating
  • Ideal for busy professionals, teens, and active lifestyles
  • Fewer check-up appointments compared to traditional braces

Modern Digital Workflow

Every aligner treatment is planned using advanced CAD/CAM software. We scan your teeth with our intraoral scanner and map out the movement of your teeth step-by-step. You will receive a series of trays to wear for about 20-22 hours a day, transitioning to the next set every 1-2 weeks.

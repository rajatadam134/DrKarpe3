our infrastructure

Fully Equipped State-of-the-Art Facilities

At Dr. Karpe's Centre for Advanced Dentistry, we strive to deliver the highest level of dental care through state-of-the-art clinical infrastructure. Every corner of our clinic is optimized for hygiene, safety, and patient convenience. Our facility is organized into four separate clinical divisions, enabling specialists to focus on specific procedures under one single roof.

OPD Unit

General dental checkups, diagnostics, and primary treatment planning.

Surgical Unit

Advanced sterile operatory theater for implantology and oral surgeries.

Child Unit

A welcoming, friendly environment tailored specifically for pediatric care.

In-house OPG Unit

Digital panoramic orthopantomogram X-rays for instant, clear diagnostics.

Hygiene protocol strictly compliant with international safety standards.

Meet Our Experienced Doctors

Our specialists coordinate diagnosis and planning to deliver optimal aesthetic and restorative treatment outcomes.

Dr. Vivek Karpe

B.D.S. (Govt Dental College Mumbai)
Implantologist

Dr. Vivek Karpe completed his BDS from the Government Dental College, Mumbai, and has over 25 years of clinical experience. Specializing in advanced implantology, he has successfully performed hundreds of procedures. He trained in the "All-on-4" implant concept in Germany and is committed to evidence-based practice.

Dr. Ashwini Karpe

B.D.S. (Govt Dental College Aurangabad)
Cosmetic Dentist

Dr. Ashwini Karpe completed her BDS from the Government Dental College, Aurangabad, and has over 20 years of clinical experience. Specializing in cosmetic dentistry, she has delivered beautiful smiles using porcelain laminates, veneers, and modern teeth whitening protocols.

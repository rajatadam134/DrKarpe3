Meet Our Experienced Doctors

Our specialists coordinate diagnosis and planning to deliver optimal aesthetic and restorative treatment outcomes.

Dr. Vivek Karpe

B.D.S. (Govt Dental College Mumbai)
Implantologist

Dr. Vivek Karpe completed his BDS from the Government Dental College, Mumbai, and has over 25 years of clinical experience. Specializing in advanced implantology, he has successfully performed hundreds of procedures. He trained in the "All-on-4" implant concept in Germany and is committed to evidence-based practice.

Dr. Ashvini Karpe

B.D.S. (Govt Dental College Aurangabad)
Cosmetic Dentist

Dr. Ashvini Karpe completed her BDS from the Government Dental College, Aurangabad, and has over 20 years of clinical experience. Specializing in cosmetic dentistry, she has delivered beautiful smiles using porcelain laminates, veneers, and modern teeth whitening protocols.

Dr. Muniza Qureshi

B.D.S. (Fellowship in Endodontics)
Endodontist

Dr. Muniza Qureshi completed her BDS in 2022 and further enhanced her clinical expertise with a fellowship in Endodontics from Hyderabad in 2024. Specializing in clinical endodontics, she focuses on painless, patient-centered care and modern minimally invasive root canal treatments. Certified in rotary endodontics, indirect restorations, aesthetic dentistry, and minor surgical procedures, she is dedicated to continuous learning.

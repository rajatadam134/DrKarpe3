Comprehensive Rehabilitation for Complex Cases

Advanced Care Treatments address complex oral health issues that affect the entire chewing system, including jaw alignment, multiple broken or missing teeth, and chronic jaw joint pain. These restorative and neuromuscular procedures aim to return your mouth to optimal function and aesthetics.

Our Advanced Care Services

We offer full-mouth rehabilitation (restoring all teeth using crowns, implants, or veneers), TMJ/TMD joint pain therapy, splint therapy to prevent tooth grinding (bruxism).

  • Resolves chronic jaw joint (TMJ) clicking and facial pain
  • Restores collapsed bites, chewing function, and aesthetics
  • Customized mouthguards to stop night-time tooth clenching
  • Advanced digital design and planning for predictable results
  • Restores dynamic muscle harmony and joint health

Multidisciplinary Planning

Complex cases require a detailed analysis of your bite, muscle activity, and jaw joints. Using advanced digital diagnostics and smile design software, our specialists map out a staged treatment plan to reconstruct a stable, comfortable, and beautiful smile.

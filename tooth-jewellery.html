Safe, Sparkly Smile Enhancements

Tooth jewellery is a popular aesthetic treatment that adds a unique sparkle to your smile. It involves bonding a small, certified crystal glass stud or gold emblem onto the surface of a front tooth. It is a completely cosmetic and non-invasive procedure.

The application process is entirely safe and painless. There is absolutely no drilling or damage to the tooth structure. The jewel is bonded to the enamel using a dental adhesive, similar to how orthodontic brackets are attached, and can be removed easily whenever desired.

Painless Application in Minutes

The entire procedure takes less than 15 minutes. We use only high-quality, certified dental crystals to ensure they are safe for oral use and do not cause irritation to the lips or gums.

  • Safe, painless application in 15 minutes
  • No drilling or damage to tooth enamel
  • Easily removable by a dentist at any time
  • Certified high-quality aesthetic crystals

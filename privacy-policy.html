Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Dr. Karpe's Centre for Advanced Dentistry (Solapur & Akkalkot), we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, safeguard, and disclose the information gathered through our website forms and interactions.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when filling out interaction forms on our website, such as our Appointment Booking Form and Workplace Dental Camp Request Form. The information collected includes:

  • Contact Details: Full Name, Email Address, and Mobile / Phone Number.
  • Appointment Specifications: Preferred Clinic Branch (Solapur Railway Line or Akkalkot Kanda Bazaar), Preferred Date, and Preferred Dental Treatment.
  • Workplace / Camp Details: Company/Organization Name and special event location requests.
  • Patient Notes: Any specific symptoms, inquiries, or dental medical notes entered into form message fields.
  • Consent Record: Explicit consent selected by you for receiving clinic communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly for legitimate clinical and administrative healthcare purposes, including:

  • Scheduling, confirming, or rescheduling dental consultation appointments.
  • Coordinating workplace dental checkup camps, health drives, and expert consultations.
  • Contacting you via Phone Call, SMS, or WhatsApp to send appointment reminders, diagnostic reports, or consultation details.
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing personalized dental health advice.
  • Improving our clinical services, website usability, and patient care standards.

3. Communications Consent (Calls, SMS & WhatsApp)

By filling out our website forms and checking the consent box, you grant Dr. Karpe's Centre for Advanced Dentistry explicit permission to communicate with you using the contact details provided.

WhatsApp & Direct Messaging Notice:

Our forms feature direct WhatsApp integration (`wa.me`) to provide instant responses. When you click submit, your submitted details are pre-formatted into a WhatsApp chat for convenience. We do not use your phone number for unsolicited marketing or spam.

Opting Out: You may withdraw your consent to receive calls or promotional/reminder messages at any time by contacting our reception desk or emailing us at privacy@drkarpe.com.

4. Data Protection & Confidentiality

We treat all patient data as strictly confidential doctor-patient information. We enforce stringent administrative, physical, and digital safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We SSL-encrypt our website traffic to ensure your information travels securely between your browser and our servers.

5. Zero Third-Party Data Selling

We do not sell, rent, trade, or lease your personal information or mobile phone numbers to third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketing agencies under any circumstances.

Information is disclosed only when required by applicable laws, legal processes, or to protect the safety and medical welfare of our patients.

6. Cookies & Web Analytics

Our website may use essential cookies and basic session analytics to ensure proper functionality, fast page loads, and smooth navigation. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some interactive elements of the website may perform differently.

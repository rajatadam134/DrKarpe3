Durable & Natural-Looking Restorations

Dental crowns and bridges are custom-made fixed prosthetics used to restore damaged, weakened, or missing teeth. A crown (or cap) encases a single damaged tooth to protect it from fracture, while a bridge spans a gap left by missing teeth, anchoring to adjacent teeth for support.

Premium Restorative Materials

We use advanced, metal-free materials like Zirconia and E-Max porcelain. These materials offer exceptional strength, biocompatibility, and translucent properties that mimic natural tooth enamel, ensuring your crown or bridge blends seamlessly with your smile.

  • Restores full biting strength and chewing efficiency
  • Protects root-canal-treated teeth from fracturing
  • Ceramic materials offer natural-looking translucency
  • Prevents remaining teeth from shifting out of alignment
  • Long-lasting, highly biocompatible prosthetic materials

Precise Digital Scanning

Using our iTero Lumina intraoral scanner, we capture high-definition 3D digital impressions of your prepared teeth. This ensures a highly accurate, comfortable fit and allows our dental laboratories to fabricate your restorations with extreme precision.

Porcelain Veneers & Smile Makeovers

Smile Designing is a custom cosmetic treatment plan designed to rebuild your confidence. Using ultra-thin porcelain laminates or veneers, we can correct minor tooth misalignments, close gaps (diastema), cover chips, and mask deep enamel staining that does not respond to whitening.

Every smile design is highly individualized. We evaluate your facial shape, lip line, gum display, and skin tone to create a balanced, beautiful smile that looks completely natural. Our high-grade porcelain materials mimic the light-reflecting properties of natural enamel, delivering outstanding aesthetics.

Minimally Invasive Artistry

Modern cosmetic dentistry focuses on preserving as much natural tooth structure as possible. Porcelain veneers require only minimal preparation (often less than 0.5mm of enamel removal), making it a safe, conservative, and comfortable procedure.

  • Corrects gaps, chips, and worn teeth
  • Provides permanent stain resistance
  • Minimally invasive with rapid turnaround
  • Creates symmetrical, natural-looking smiles

Frequently Asked Questions

A veneer is an ultra-thin shell that covers only the front surface of the tooth to improve aesthetics. A crown covers the entire tooth structure and is used when a tooth is severely decayed or broken to restore its strength.

With good oral hygiene and regular dental checkups, porcelain veneers typically last between 10 to 15 years, and often much longer.

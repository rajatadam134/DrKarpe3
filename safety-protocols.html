patient safety first

Gold Standard in Disinfection & Sterilisation

We follow a strict 4-step sterilisation protocol for every instrument to guarantee absolute safety, hygiene, and patient transparency.

Step 1

Precleaning & Disinfection

Instruments are immersed in a medical-grade disinfectant solution for at least 30 minutes immediately after use to reduce pathogenic load.

Step 2

Ultrasonic Cleaning

High-frequency sound waves in an ultrasonic chamber remove micro-elements, debris, and bio-burden from hidden hinges for deep cleaning.

Step 3

Class-B Autoclaving

Instruments are sealed in pouches and undergo vacuum steam cycles under high pressure in a Class-B autoclave to ensure complete sterilisation.

Step 4

UV Protection & Transparency

Sterilised pouches are stored in UV cabinets to keep them sterile until use, and are opened directly in front of each patient for absolute transparency.

quality assurance

The Gold Standard in Quality Assurance

Compliance, Audits, and Emergency Readiness — No Compromise. We adhere to rigorous standards to ensure your safety and comfort at every step of your clinical journey.

Strict Guidelines & Compliance

We operate in strict compliance with safety directives laid down by the World Health Organization (WHO), Center for Disease Control (CDC), and the Indian Dental Association (IDA).

Regular Sterilization Audits

Weekly biological spore testing and chemical indicators are utilized to audit autoclave efficiency, ensuring complete compliance with sterilisation quality standards.

Emergency Medical Readiness

Our facility is equipped with dedicated medical oxygen cylinders, essential emergency drug kits, emergency action protocols, and certified staff trained in CPR/first aid.
